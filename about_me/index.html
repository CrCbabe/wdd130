About Me

When it comes to my education, I am planning to major in computer science. I hope to be able to work in the video game industry, be it as a designer, an entertainer, or as a voice actor. By taking this class in web design, I believe I can greater understand how to design things that are efficient, as well as aesthetically pleasing, for both websites and in general.
