Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the Dry Oar site will be to promote the Dry Oar whitewater rafting business. Dry Oar is committed to providing a positive experience for whitewater rafters of all ages and interest levels, especially within family and friend settings. With a webiste, we intend to make our services at Dry Oar more easily accessible for potential patrons.

Audience

Dry Oar is targeted towards families searching for a whitewater rafting experience. A lot of young adults enjoy whitewater rafting, and many may desire to share such a passion with a family or with friends. We at Dry Oar intend to deliver a safe yet exhilarating whitewater rafting experience. This website will typically be accessed through a laptop, and sometimes through mobile devices.
